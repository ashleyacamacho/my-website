Practice = Prosperity

Become a better athlete, designer, speaker, et cetera by taking in these tips and running with them; the destination is your choice.

It is Always the Journey Not The Destination

First and Foremost: Consistency is Key!

Stay consistent. If you need to stretch or study everyday in order to reach your ultimate goal, then do it. It is ok okay to take a break every now and then but do not slack. That is if you want to be successful.

Make A Plan

Cooking goes best when you have a recipe at hand, right? Some thing goes for you and your goals. Plan what you need to have or get done in order to reach that specific goal. If you do not exactlty have a plan in mind it is okay, you will figure it out along the way. :)

If You're Not Motivated, Find What Motivates You

Everyone is motivated by different things. Some people tend to watch motivational videos online. Others would rather actually go out and physically see what other's hardships are to see the brighter side in their own lives.

It Won't Happen Overnight

Everything that it is worth it in the end will take time.

Do It For You

Do not do let others and society influence the choices you make for yourself. Do not just do it for the money, do it because you love it.
